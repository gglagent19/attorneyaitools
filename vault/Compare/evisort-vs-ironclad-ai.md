# Evisort vs Ironclad AI: Which CLM Platform Wins for Enterprise Legal

Evisort and Ironclad AI are the two most discussed contract lifecycle management platforms for enterprise legal teams. Both wrap AI around the full contract journey, from intake through negotiation, signature, and post-execution analytics. Each took a distinct path to get here, and those differences matter when you evaluate fit.

## Quick Verdict

Ironclad AI is the better choice for teams that want a modern workflow-first CLM with deep sales integrations and a polished user experience. Evisort is the stronger choice for teams that prioritize AI-driven contract analytics, repository intelligence, and retroactive extraction across legacy contract populations. Ironclad wins on workflow, Evisort wins on intelligence.

## When to Choose Evisort

Evisort is the right choice when your legal team already has thousands of executed contracts sitting in folders and you need to understand what is actually in them. Evisort's AI can retroactively extract key terms, renewal dates, auto-renewals, and liability caps across entire contract populations. For legal ops teams tasked with risk reporting, Evisort is the gold standard.

## When to Choose Ironclad AI

Ironclad AI is the right choice when your bottleneck is the intake-to-signature workflow. Ironclad's workflow builder lets legal ops teams design no-code approval flows, route contracts to the right reviewers, and integrate tightly with Salesforce for sales-driven contract volume. Ironclad AI now layers generative drafting and clause suggestion on top of this workflow backbone.

## Pricing Breakdown

Both platforms use enterprise pricing negotiated per deployment. **Evisort** typically starts around $40,000 per year for a mid-sized team and scales into the low hundreds of thousands for global deployments. **Ironclad AI** typically starts around $35,000 per year and scales similarly. Both offer volume discounts and multi-year commitments.

## Final Recommendation

If your legal team's top priority is understanding what is in your contracts, Evisort wins. If your top priority is moving contracts through a standardized workflow with sales, procurement, and HR stakeholders, Ironclad AI wins. Some Fortune 500 legal teams run both: Ironclad for active workflow, Evisort for repository intelligence. For most companies, pick one and commit.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can Evisort handle workflow?**
Yes, Evisort has a workflow module, but it is less mature than Ironclad's.

**Can Ironclad extract data from legacy contracts?**
Yes, Ironclad offers AI extraction, but Evisort's engine is generally more accurate on messy legacy data.

**Which is better for procurement contracts?**
Ironclad has the edge thanks to deeper procurement workflow integrations.

**How long does implementation take?**
Both typically require 6-12 weeks including configuration, integration, and migration.

**Do either integrate with SAP Ariba?**
Yes, both offer native or certified integrations with major procurement suites.
