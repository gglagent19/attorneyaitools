# EvenUp vs CaseMark AI: Which Personal Injury AI Platform Wins

EvenUp and CaseMark AI are the two most prominent AI platforms built specifically for personal injury law. Both focus on medical record review, demand letter drafting, and damages modeling to help plaintiff firms settle cases faster and for more money. They take slightly different approaches, and the right pick depends on your firm's case volume and workflow.

## Quick Verdict

EvenUp is the more mature and widely adopted platform, with a strong track record of elevating settlement values for plaintiff firms. CaseMark AI is a strong challenger focused on deeper medical record analysis and faster turnaround. For high-volume PI firms, EvenUp is the industry standard; for firms wanting newer AI-first technology, CaseMark is worth a serious look.

## When to Choose EvenUp

EvenUp is the right pick for established plaintiff firms handling high volumes of soft-tissue and motor vehicle cases. EvenUp's hybrid approach, combining AI extraction with human review, produces polished demand letters that consistently beat in-house drafts on both speed and value. The platform's damages modeling is proprietary and benchmarked against thousands of prior settlements.

## When to Choose CaseMark AI

CaseMark AI is the right pick for firms that want faster turnaround and deeper AI-driven medical record analysis. CaseMark's pure AI approach delivers drafts in days instead of a week, and its medical timeline tools are especially useful for complex catastrophic injury cases. Subscription pricing can be more predictable for firms with consistent case flow.

## Pricing Breakdown

**EvenUp** charges per case, typically starting around $500 and scaling with case complexity and medical record volume. High-volume firms negotiate volume discounts. **CaseMark AI** uses a hybrid subscription plus per-case model, with subscriptions starting around $299 per month and additional per-case fees. Firms with high volume often find CaseMark's hybrid pricing more predictable.

## Final Recommendation

For high-volume plaintiff firms with established settlement workflows, EvenUp remains the industry standard and the safer bet. For firms seeking faster turnaround, predictable pricing, and modern AI-first tooling, CaseMark AI is a strong alternative worth piloting. Some firms use EvenUp for high-value cases and CaseMark for bulk soft-tissue cases.
