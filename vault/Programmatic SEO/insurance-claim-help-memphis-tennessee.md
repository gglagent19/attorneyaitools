## Turning a Memphis, Tennessee denial around: the steps that work

1. **Read the denial or estimate closely.** Pin down the exact policy provision your insurer leaned on for this insurance claim.
2. **Document everything** in Memphis, Tennessee — dated photos, video, receipts, and a written timeline of the loss.
