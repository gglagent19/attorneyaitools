## Turning a Sacramento, California denial around: the steps that work

1. **Decode the denial.** Find the specific exclusion or scope item the adjuster cited on your Sacramento, California claim.
2. **Document everything** in Sacramento, California — dated photos, video, receipts, and a written timeline of the loss.
3. **Get an independent estimate** from a licensed California contractor — the gap between their scope and the adjuster's is your leverage.
4. **Request a re-inspection in writing** and submit an itemized rebuttal that ties each disputed item to your policy and your evidence.
