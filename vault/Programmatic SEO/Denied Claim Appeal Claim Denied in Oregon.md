## How to dispute a denied claim claim in Oregon

1. **Read the denial or estimate closely.** Pin down the exact policy provision your insurer leaned on for this denied claim claim.
2. **Document everything** in Oregon — dated photos, video, receipts, and a written timeline of the loss.
