## Turning a Hartford, Connecticut denial around: the steps that work

1. **Decode the denial.** Find the specific exclusion or scope item the adjuster cited on your Hartford, Connecticut claim.
2. **Document everything** in Hartford, Connecticut — dated photos, video, receipts, and a written timeline of the loss.
3. **Get an independent estimate** from a licensed Connecticut contractor — the gap between their scope and the adjuster's is your leverage.
4. **Request a re-inspection in writing** and submit an itemized rebuttal that ties each disputed item to your policy and your evidence.
5. **Take it higher** — file with the Connecticut Department of Insurance ([find it here](https://content.naic.org/state-insurance-departments)), or invoke your policy's appraisal provision for amount disputes.
