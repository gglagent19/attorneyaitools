Adjusters working Suffolk County handle everything from storm losses to water and fire damage. Common reasons a Boston, Massachusetts claim is denied or underpaid:

## Turning a Boston, Massachusetts denial around: the steps that work

1. **Decode the denial.** Find the specific exclusion or scope item the adjuster cited on your Boston, Massachusetts claim.
2. **Document everything** in Boston, Massachusetts — dated photos, video, receipts, and a written timeline of the loss.
3. **Get an independent estimate** from a licensed Massachusetts contractor — the gap between their scope and the adjuster's is your leverage.
4. **Request a re-inspection in writing** and submit an itemized rebuttal that ties each disputed item to your policy and your evidence.
5. **Take it higher** — file with the Massachusetts Department of Insurance ([find it here](https://content.naic.org/state-insurance-departments)), or invoke your policy's appraisal provision for amount disputes.

> **Watch the clock.** Your Boston, Massachusetts policy almost certainly has a "suit limitation" clause and a prompt-notice requirement. Verify both against your own contract and the Massachusetts Department of Insurance before they cost you the claim.
