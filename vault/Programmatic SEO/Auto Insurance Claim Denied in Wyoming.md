## Why Auto Insurance Claims Get Denied in Wyoming

Across Wyoming, auto claims are denied or trimmed for a predictable set of reasons:

- The vehicle was declared a total loss at an actual cash value below comparable local listings
- Diminished value after repairs was ignored
- OEM parts were swapped for aftermarket parts in the estimate
- Injury or rental coverage was underpaid or delayed

In Wyoming, where hail, wind, and blizzards drive a large share of property losses, auto claims are especially prone to causation disputes — insurers may attribute the damage to an excluded cause to reduce or deny payment.

## Fighting a auto claim in Wyoming, step by step

2. **Document everything** in Wyoming — dated photos, video, receipts, and a written timeline of the loss.
