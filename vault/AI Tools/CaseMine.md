# CaseMine

CaseMine uses its proprietary CaseIQ technology to help attorneys discover relevant case law they might otherwise miss. By analyzing uploaded documents or search queries, CaseIQ identifies conceptually similar cases across jurisdictions, going beyond keyword matching to understand legal concepts and principles. The platform covers case law from multiple countries including the US, UK, India, and Canada.

## Features
- CaseIQ document analysis for finding relevant authorities
- Conceptual search beyond keyword matching
- Multi-jurisdictional coverage (US, UK, India, Canada, and more)
- Case relationship mapping and visualization
- AI-generated case summaries and headnotes

## Best For
- Attorneys handling matters with authorities across multiple jurisdictions
- Researchers looking for conceptually related cases beyond keyword matches

## Pricing
Free tier with limited searches per month. Pro plan at $49/month with unlimited searches. Team plans starting at $39/month per user for 5+ users.
