# Docket Alarm

Docket Alarm, now part of Fastcase, provides comprehensive docket search and litigation analytics across federal and state courts. The platform uses AI to analyze docket entries, predict case outcomes, and alert attorneys to relevant filings. Its database includes millions of dockets with full-text searchable documents, making it a valuable tool for litigation research and competitive intelligence.

## Features
- Full-text search across millions of court dockets
- Real-time docket alerts and monitoring
- AI-powered case outcome predictions
- Brief and motion search from court filings
- PTAB and ITC analytics for patent practitioners

## Best For
- Litigators monitoring case developments across jurisdictions
- Patent attorneys tracking PTAB proceedings and outcomes

## Pricing
Basic monitoring plan from $49/month. Research plan with full analytics at $149/month. Enterprise licensing available for firms and legal departments.
