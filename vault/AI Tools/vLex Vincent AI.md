# vLex Vincent AI

vLex Vincent AI is a generative AI legal research assistant that leverages vLex's massive global legal database covering over 100 jurisdictions. Vincent can answer complex legal questions, summarize cases, and identify relevant authorities across multiple legal systems. It is particularly strong for international and comparative law research.

## Features
- AI-powered research across 100+ jurisdictions
- Multi-language legal analysis and translation
- Case summarization and legal concept extraction
- Cross-referencing between jurisdictions for comparative analysis
- Integration with vLex's database of 130+ million legal documents

## Best For

- Attorneys needing comparative law research across jurisdictions
