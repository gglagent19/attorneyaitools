# DoNotPay

DoNotPay bills itself as "the world's first robot lawyer," helping consumers fight back against corporations and bureaucracy. The AI-powered platform automates common legal tasks such as disputing parking tickets, claiming refunds, canceling subscriptions, and filing small claims court paperwork. While not a tool for practicing attorneys, it demonstrates AI's potential to democratize legal access.

## Features
- Automated parking ticket disputes and appeals
- Subscription cancellation automation
- Demand letter generation
- Small claims court filing assistance
- Consumer rights claim automation

## Best For
- Consumers dealing with everyday legal and administrative hassles
- Individuals who cannot afford traditional legal representation for minor matters

## Pricing
Free trial available. Standard plan at $36 for 3 months. Covers unlimited use of all features during the subscription period.
