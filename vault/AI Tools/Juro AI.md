# Juro AI

Juro is a modern contract collaboration platform designed for the way business actually works. Unlike traditional CLM tools that rely on Word documents and email chains, Juro provides a browser-native contract editor with AI assistance. Business teams can self-serve routine contracts from approved templates, while legal maintains control through AI-powered review and approval workflows.

## Features
- Browser-native rich-text contract editor
- AI-assisted contract review and clause suggestions
- Self-serve contract workflows for business teams
- Automated data extraction from signed contracts
- Real-time collaboration with commenting and approvals

## Best For
- Legal teams wanting to empower business users with self-serve contracts
- Companies tired of managing contracts through Word and email

## Pricing
Team plan starting at $1,500/month for up to 10 users. Scale plan with advanced AI features from $3,000/month. Enterprise pricing for larger deployments.
