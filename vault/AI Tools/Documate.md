# Documate

Documate is a document automation platform with a strong focus on access to justice. The platform enables legal aid organizations, courts, and pro bono programs to create guided interviews that walk users through the process of generating legal documents. Now part of Gavel, Documate continues to serve organizations committed to making legal services more accessible.

## Features
- Guided interview builder for document generation
- Conditional branching based on user responses
- Multi-language support for diverse communities
- Embeddable forms for court and legal aid websites
- Analytics on document generation and user completion rates

## Best For
- Legal aid organizations building self-help tools
- Courts creating guided form-filling experiences for self-represented litigants

## Pricing
Free for qualifying legal aid and pro bono organizations. Professional plan at $59/month. Organization plan at $149/month with advanced features.
