# Evisort

Evisort is an AI-native contract intelligence platform that transforms unstructured contract data into actionable insights. The platform automatically ingests, classifies, and extracts key terms from contracts, creating a searchable, intelligent repository. Its AI continuously learns from user interactions to improve extraction accuracy over time.

## Features
- Automatic contract ingestion and AI classification
- Extraction of 50+ standard clause types out of the box
- Custom AI model training for organization-specific terms
- Contract analytics dashboards and reporting
- Workflow automation for renewals and obligations

## Best For
- Legal operations teams building a centralized contract intelligence hub
- CFOs and procurement teams seeking visibility into contractual commitments

## Pricing
Professional plan starting at $3,000/month. Enterprise plan with custom AI models and advanced analytics available. Volume-based pricing for large portfolios.
