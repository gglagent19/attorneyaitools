# Spellbook AI

Spellbook is an AI contract drafting tool that integrates directly into Microsoft Word, where most lawyers already work. Powered by large language models fine-tuned on legal data, Spellbook can suggest new clauses, identify missing provisions, flag aggressive terms, and even draft entire contract sections based on context. It understands legal concepts and adapts suggestions to the specific type of agreement being drafted.

## Features
- Real-time AI clause suggestions within Microsoft Word
- Missing provision detection and gap analysis
- Aggressive or unusual term flagging
- Full clause and section drafting from natural language prompts
- Trained on billions of data points from legal contracts

## Best For
- Transactional attorneys drafting and reviewing contracts daily
- Solo practitioners and small firms needing drafting efficiency

## Pricing
Individual plan at $99/month. Team plan at $79/month per user for 5+ users. Enterprise pricing with custom training available.
