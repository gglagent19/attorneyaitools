# Blue J Legal

Blue J Legal uses machine learning to predict the outcomes of tax and employment law cases. The platform analyzes thousands of judicial decisions to identify patterns and predict how courts will rule on specific fact patterns. Attorneys can input their client's facts and receive a prediction with a confidence score, along with the most relevant authorities.

## Features
- AI outcome prediction for tax law scenarios
- Confidence scoring based on analyzed judicial patterns
- Employment classification analysis (contractor vs. employee)
- Scenario comparison for tax planning strategies
- Integration with major tax research platforms

## Best For
- Tax attorneys planning client strategies based on predicted outcomes
- Employment lawyers assessing worker classification risks

## Pricing
Individual plans at $150/month for single practice area. Multi-area plans at $250/month. Firm-wide licensing available with volume pricing.
