# AI Lawyer

AI Lawyer is an accessible AI legal assistant designed for both lawyers and the general public. It provides quick answers to legal questions, can analyze uploaded legal documents, and generates basic legal documents like NDAs and simple contracts. While not a replacement for professional legal advice, it serves as a useful first-pass tool for understanding legal situations.

## Features
- Instant answers to common legal questions
- Document upload and AI-powered analysis
- Basic legal document generation (NDAs, contracts, letters)
- Multi-language support for international users
- Case law reference suggestions

## Best For
- Individuals seeking to understand their legal rights quickly
- Small business owners needing basic legal document templates

## Pricing
Free tier with 5 queries per day. Weekly plan at $9.99. Monthly plan at $29.99 with unlimited queries and document generation. Annual plan at $199.99.
