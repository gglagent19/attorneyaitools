# Judicial Analytics AI

Judicial Analytics AI provides granular insights into how individual judges handle cases, rule on motions, and manage their dockets. The platform uses natural language processing to analyze judicial opinions and extract patterns that help attorneys tailor their arguments and strategy to specific judges. It covers both federal and state courts with varying depth of coverage.

## Features
- Individual judge profiling with detailed ruling analysis
- Motion outcome prediction by judge and issue type
- Judicial writing style analysis for tailoring briefs
- Docket management pattern identification
- Comparative analytics across judges within a district

## Best For
- Trial attorneys preparing for hearings before unfamiliar judges
- Litigation teams developing judge-specific strategy

Basic plan at $75/month for single-jurisdiction access. Multi-jurisdiction plan at $175/month. Firm-wide licensing available at custom pricing.
