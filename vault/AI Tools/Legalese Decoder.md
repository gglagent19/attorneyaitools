# Legalese Decoder

Legalese Decoder uses AI to break down complex legal language into plain English. Whether it is a dense contract, a court opinion, or terms of service, the tool provides clear, readable translations that make legal content accessible. It is popular with both legal professionals who want quick summaries and consumers trying to understand legal documents.

## Features
- AI-powered translation of legal jargon to plain English
- Document upload and batch processing
- Section-by-section breakdown of contracts
- Risk flagging for concerning clauses
- Browser extension for on-the-fly legal text translation

## Best For
- Paralegals and legal assistants summarizing documents for clients
- Non-lawyers trying to understand contracts and legal agreements

## Pricing
Free tier with 3 document translations per month. Pro plan at $19/month with unlimited translations. Business plan at $49/month with API access and team features.
