# Lexion

Lexion takes a user-first approach to AI contract management by integrating directly with the tools teams already use every day. Rather than forcing adoption of yet another platform, Lexion works within email, Slack, Google Drive, and other familiar environments. Its AI automatically extracts and tracks key contract terms, dates, and obligations.

## Features
- Deep integration with Gmail, Slack, Google Drive, and Salesforce
- AI-powered contract data extraction and tracking
- Automated renewal and expiration alerts
- Natural language search across contract repository
- Request intake workflows for business stakeholders

## Best For
- Mid-market companies wanting contract management without heavy implementation
- Teams that prefer working within their existing tool ecosystem

## Pricing
Starter plan from $1,000/month for small teams. Growth plan from $2,500/month with advanced AI and integrations. Enterprise pricing available.
