# Precisely

Precisely takes a unique approach to contract management by focusing on contract creation through guided Q&A workflows. Instead of starting from templates that users might edit incorrectly, Precisely guides users through a series of questions and automatically generates compliant contracts based on their answers. This ensures every contract meets legal standards without requiring legal review.

## Features
- Q&A-driven contract generation workflows
- AI-powered clause selection based on user responses
- Automatic compliance with company legal policies
- Multi-language contract generation
- Analytics on contract creation patterns and bottlenecks

## Best For
- Organizations wanting to enable self-service contract creation
- Legal teams seeking to reduce review burden for routine agreements

## Pricing
Starter plan from $800/month for up to 5 users. Professional plan from $2,000/month. Enterprise pricing with custom workflows available.
