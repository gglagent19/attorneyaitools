# Everlaw AI

Everlaw is a cloud-native e-discovery platform known for its intuitive interface and transparent AI. The platform's predictive coding implementation emphasizes explainability, showing reviewers why the AI classified documents in a particular way. Everlaw also offers strong collaboration features, making it popular with litigation teams that span multiple organizations.

## Features
- Transparent predictive coding with explainable AI decisions
- Storybuilder for organizing key evidence narratives
- Cross-matter analytics for leveraging prior work product
- Real-time collaboration across firms and organizations
- Native cloud architecture with FedRAMP authorization

## Best For
- Government agencies and contractors needing FedRAMP-authorized e-discovery
- Litigation teams valuing AI transparency and explainability

## Pricing
Subscription-based pricing from $25/GB/month. Project-based pricing available. Government pricing with FedRAMP compliance. Contact sales for quotes.
