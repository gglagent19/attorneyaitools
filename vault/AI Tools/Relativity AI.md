# Relativity AI

Relativity is the dominant platform in e-discovery, used by most major law firms and corporations for document review and litigation support. Its AI capabilities include Active Learning (predictive coding), email threading, conceptual analytics, and sentiment analysis. RelativityOne, its cloud-based offering, brings these capabilities to a secure, scalable SaaS environment.

## Features
- Active Learning (predictive coding) for prioritized document review
- AI-powered conceptual analytics and clustering
- Email threading and communication analysis
- Structured analytics for near-duplicate identification
- RelativityOne cloud platform with elastic scalability

## Best For
- Large law firms and service providers handling complex e-discovery matters
- Corporate legal departments managing internal investigations

## Pricing
RelativityOne pricing based on data volume, starting around $18/GB/month. Server licensing available for on-premise deployment. Contact for enterprise pricing.
