# Rally Legal

Rally Legal simplifies the contract drafting and negotiation process with AI-enhanced collaborative tools. The platform provides smart templates that adapt based on deal parameters, real-time collaboration for legal teams, and AI-powered suggestions for improving contract language. Rally focuses on making the drafting-to-signature process faster and more transparent.

## Features
- Smart contract templates with conditional logic
- Real-time collaborative editing and redlining
- AI-powered clause library with contextual suggestions
- Version comparison and change tracking
- E-signature integration for execution

## Best For
- In-house legal teams managing high volumes of similar contracts
- Business attorneys looking to streamline negotiation cycles

## Pricing
Starter plan at $49/month per user. Professional plan at $99/month per user with advanced AI features. Enterprise pricing available.
