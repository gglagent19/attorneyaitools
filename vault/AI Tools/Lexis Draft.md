# Lexis Draft

Lexis Draft is a Microsoft Word add-in from LexisNexis that assists attorneys during the document drafting process. It automatically checks citations for accuracy, verifies that defined terms are used consistently, identifies potential errors, and helps format documents to court-specific standards. The tool connects to the Lexis+ database for real-time citation verification.

## Features
- Real-time citation checking and Shepard's signal integration
- Defined term consistency verification
- Court-specific formatting templates
- Section and cross-reference validation
- Direct access to Lexis+ research from within Word

## Best For
- Litigators finalizing briefs and motions for court filing
- Associates responsible for cite-checking and proofreading

## Pricing
Included with certain Lexis+ subscription tiers. Standalone pricing at $75/month per user. Volume discounts for firms.
