# Casetext AI

Casetext, now part of Thomson Reuters, combines traditional legal research with its CoCounsel AI assistant. The platform provides comprehensive access to case law, statutes, and regulations while leveraging AI to surface the most relevant authorities. Its CARA AI technology analyzes uploaded briefs to find on-point cases that keyword searches miss.

## Features
- CoCounsel AI assistant for conversational legal research
- CARA AI brief analyzer for finding relevant authorities
- Comprehensive federal and state case law database
- Smart citation analysis and Shepardizing equivalent
- Parallel search for simultaneous multi-database queries

## Best For
- Solo practitioners and small firms seeking affordable AI-powered research
- Litigators needing rapid brief analysis and authority verification

## Pricing
Starts at $110/month per user for the basic plan. CoCounsel AI features available at $250/month per user. Enterprise pricing available for firms with 10+ users.
