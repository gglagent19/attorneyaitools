# Lexis+ AI

Lexis+ AI brings generative AI capabilities to the LexisNexis legal research ecosystem. It allows attorneys to ask legal questions in natural language and receive AI-generated responses grounded in LexisNexis's vast database of case law, statutes, regulations, and secondary sources. Every AI response includes linked citations that can be verified against primary sources.

## Features
- Conversational AI search grounded in Lexis+ content
- Linked, verifiable citations in every response
- Document drafting and summarization tools
- Timeline and chronology generation from case documents
- Integration with Lexis+ research workflow

## Best For
- Firms already invested in the LexisNexis ecosystem seeking AI augmentation
- Attorneys who prioritize citation accuracy and verifiability

## Pricing
Available as an add-on to existing Lexis+ subscriptions. Pricing varies by firm size and package. Typically $150-$400/month per user depending on tier. Contact LexisNexis for a custom quote.
