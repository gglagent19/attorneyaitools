# Luminance

Luminance has developed proprietary Legal Large Language Models specifically trained for the legal profession. The platform handles the entire contract lifecycle from review through negotiation, using AI to read, understand, and even negotiate contracts autonomously. Its technology is used by law firms and corporate legal teams across 60+ countries for due diligence, contract management, and compliance.

## Features
- Proprietary Legal LLM trained on millions of legal documents
- Autopilot mode for autonomous contract negotiation
- Due diligence analysis with anomaly detection
- Multi-language contract analysis (80+ languages)
- Real-time collaboration and approval workflows

## Best For
- Large law firms handling cross-border M&A due diligence
- Legal departments seeking autonomous contract negotiation capabilities

## Pricing
Enterprise pricing based on transaction volume. Typical annual contracts range from $75,000-$300,000. Free pilot available for qualifying organizations.
