# Kira Systems

Kira Systems is a market leader in AI-powered contract analysis. The platform uses advanced machine learning to automatically identify and extract relevant provisions from contracts, dramatically accelerating due diligence, M&A reviews, and lease abstraction. Kira can recognize over 1,000 built-in provision types and can be trained to identify custom provisions specific to a firm's needs.

## Features
- Automatic extraction of 1,000+ built-in provision types
- Custom machine learning model training for firm-specific provisions
- Bulk contract analysis for portfolio-wide reviews
- Side-by-side comparison of extracted terms
- Integration with document management systems and deal rooms

## Best For
- M&A teams conducting large-scale due diligence reviews
- Corporate legal departments managing contract portfolios
