# Ironclad AI

Ironclad is a digital contracting platform that uses AI to streamline the entire contract lifecycle. From creation through negotiation, approval, execution, and storage, Ironclad's AI assists at every stage. Its AI Assist feature helps draft contracts, extract key terms, and ensure compliance with company policies, while its workflow engine automates approval routing.

## Features
- AI-powered contract drafting from customizable templates
- Intelligent metadata extraction and contract indexing
- Automated approval workflow routing
- AI Assist for clause suggestions and risk identification
- Integration with Salesforce, Slack, and other business tools

## Best For
- Fast-growing companies needing scalable contract processes
- Legal operations teams modernizing contract workflows

## Pricing
Starter plan available for small teams. Professional and Enterprise tiers with increasing AI capabilities. Pricing starts around $3,000/month. Contact sales for specific quotes.
