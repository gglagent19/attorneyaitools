# LawGeex

LawGeex automates the contract review process by comparing incoming contracts against a company's predefined legal policies and playbooks. The AI identifies deviations from approved terms, flags risky clauses, and suggests redlines, enabling legal teams to approve routine contracts in minutes rather than days. Studies have shown LawGeex achieves 94% accuracy in NDA review, outperforming many human reviewers.

## Features
- Automated contract review against custom playbooks
- AI-generated redlines and markup suggestions
- Risk scoring for contract clauses
- Approval workflow automation
- Integration with CLM platforms and e-signature tools

## Best For
- In-house legal teams drowning in routine contract review requests
- Companies seeking to standardize contract terms across business units
