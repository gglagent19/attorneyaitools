# Trellis Law

Trellis Law provides access to millions of state trial court records that are typically difficult to find online. The platform uses AI to analyze judicial rulings, extract key holdings, and surface patterns in how specific judges handle certain types of motions and cases. This intelligence is invaluable for litigators who need to understand their judge before stepping into the courtroom.

## Features
- Searchable database of state trial court records
- AI-powered judicial ruling analysis and extraction
- Motion outcome analytics by judge
- Case trend identification across courts and time periods
- Judicial profile generation with ruling history

## Best For
- Trial attorneys researching their assigned judge's tendencies
- Litigation teams preparing motion strategy with data-driven insights

## Pricing
Solo plan at $99/month. Team plan at $79/month per user for 5+ seats. Enterprise and statewide access packages available.
