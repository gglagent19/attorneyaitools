# LinkSquares

LinkSquares provides a comprehensive contract lifecycle platform that covers both pre-signature and post-signature needs. Its AI engine, trained on millions of legal agreements, automatically extracts over 100 data points from contracts and provides powerful analytics. The platform bridges the gap between contract creation and ongoing contract intelligence.

## Features
- AI extraction of 100+ contract data points
- Pre-signature drafting and collaboration tools
- Post-signature analytics and reporting dashboards
- Smart search across entire contract repository
- Automated obligation and renewal tracking

## Best For
- General counsel needing visibility into the full contract portfolio
- Legal ops teams building comprehensive contract intelligence programs

## Pricing
Analyze (post-signature) starting at $2,500/month. Finalize (pre-signature) starting at $2,000/month. Combined platform pricing available. Enterprise quotes for large deployments.
