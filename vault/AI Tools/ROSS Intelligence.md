# ROSS Intelligence

ROSS Intelligence was one of the pioneering AI legal research platforms, leveraging natural language processing to let attorneys ask legal questions in plain English. The platform searches through case law to find the most relevant authorities and provides concise answers with supporting citations. While it faced legal challenges from Westlaw, ROSS has continued to evolve its technology.

## Features
- Natural language question-and-answer legal research
- AI-ranked search results based on relevance
- Case law passage highlighting and extraction
- Saved search and alert notifications
- Brief analysis for identifying relevant authorities

## Best For
- Junior associates conducting preliminary legal research
- Small firms looking for an intuitive alternative to traditional research platforms

## Pricing
Plans start at $89/month for individual users. Team plans available at $69/month per user for groups of 5+. Academic pricing available for law schools.
