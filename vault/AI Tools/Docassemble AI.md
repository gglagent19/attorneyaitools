# Docassemble AI

Docassemble is a free, open-source expert system for guided interviews and document assembly, now enhanced with AI integration capabilities. Used extensively by courts, legal aid organizations, and law schools, it allows developers to build sophisticated legal applications that guide users through complex decision trees and generate customized documents. Its AI integrations enable natural language processing and intelligent routing.

## Features
- Open-source platform with active developer community
- Python-based interview logic for maximum flexibility
- AI/LLM integration for intelligent question routing
- Multi-format document output (PDF, DOCX, HTML)
- SMS and email notification capabilities

## Best For
- Legal technologists building custom document automation applications
- Courts and legal aid organizations with developer resources

## Pricing
Free and open source. Hosted solutions available from third parties starting at $35/month. Enterprise support packages available.
