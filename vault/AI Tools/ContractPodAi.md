# ContractPodAi

ContractPodAi provides an end-to-end contract lifecycle management solution powered by AI. The platform handles everything from contract creation to obligation management, using AI to extract data from existing contracts, identify risks, and track key dates and obligations. It integrates with Microsoft ecosystem tools and is built to serve enterprise legal departments.

## Features
- AI-powered contract data extraction and classification
- Obligation tracking with automated reminders
- Template-based contract generation
- Risk analysis and clause comparison
- Microsoft 365 and Teams integration

## Best For
- Enterprise legal departments managing thousands of active contracts
- Organizations migrating legacy paper contracts to digital management

## Pricing
Enterprise pricing starting at $40,000/year. Mid-market plans available. Custom quotes based on contract volume and user count.
