# CourtListener AI

CourtListener is a free legal research platform operated by the non-profit Free Law Project. It provides access to millions of court opinions, oral arguments, and judicial records, enhanced with AI-powered search and analysis. The RECAP extension, which crowdsources PACER documents, has created one of the largest free collections of federal court filings available.

## Features
- Free search across millions of court opinions
- RECAP archive of crowdsourced PACER documents
- Oral argument audio recordings and transcripts
- Judicial financial disclosure database
- API access for building legal technology applications

## Best For
- Attorneys and researchers needing free access to court records
- Legal technology developers building applications on court data

## Pricing
Completely free. Supported by donations and grants. API access available with rate limits for free users, higher limits for supporters.
