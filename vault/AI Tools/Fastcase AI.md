# Fastcase AI

Fastcase is a legal research platform that provides free access to millions of legal documents through partnerships with state and local bar associations. Its AI features include intelligent search ranking, citation analysis, and interactive visual maps of legal authority. Fastcase merged with Casemaker to create the largest free legal research library available to bar members.

## Features
- Free access through 50+ bar association partnerships
- AI-powered relevance ranking of search results
- Interactive Authority Check citation analysis
- Visual mapping of case relationships and citations
- Comprehensive federal and state case law coverage

## Best For
- Solo practitioners and small firms seeking cost-effective legal research
- Bar association members wanting AI-enhanced free research tools

## Pricing
Free for members of participating bar associations. Premium plans with advanced AI features start at $65/month. Enterprise plans available for larger firms.
