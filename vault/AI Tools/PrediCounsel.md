# PrediCounsel

PrediCounsel uses advanced machine learning models to predict litigation outcomes and estimate potential damages. By analyzing patterns across historical case data, the platform provides attorneys and corporate counsel with data-driven risk assessments that inform settlement negotiations, case strategy, and litigation budgeting. The tool is especially useful for portfolio litigation management.

## Features
- AI case outcome prediction with confidence intervals
- Damages estimation based on comparable cases
- Settlement range recommendations
- Portfolio litigation risk assessment
- Scenario modeling for strategic decision-making

## Best For
- Corporate counsel managing litigation portfolios and reserves
- Insurance companies evaluating claim exposure and settlement values

## Pricing
Standard plan at $200/month with limited predictions. Professional plan at $500/month with unlimited access. Enterprise pricing for high-volume users.
