# Lex Machina

Lex Machina, a LexisNexis company, pioneered the field of legal analytics. The platform mines millions of court records to provide attorneys with data-driven insights on how judges rule, how long cases take, what damages are awarded, and how opposing counsel typically litigate. This intelligence allows legal teams to make strategic decisions based on empirical data rather than anecdote.

## Features
- Judge analytics with ruling patterns and timing data
- Attorney and law firm performance benchmarking
- Case outcome predictions based on historical data
- Damages analytics across case types and jurisdictions
- Custom analytics dashboards and reporting

## Best For
- Litigation partners developing case strategy based on data
- Business development teams pitching clients with competitive intelligence
