# Westlaw Edge AI

Westlaw Edge AI integrates artificial intelligence throughout the Westlaw research experience. From AI-powered search algorithms that understand legal concepts to predictive litigation analytics, the platform helps attorneys research more efficiently and make data-driven strategic decisions. Its Quick Check feature analyzes briefs to identify missed authorities and weaknesses.

## Features
- AI-powered natural language search with legal concept understanding
- Quick Check brief analysis for identifying gaps in authority
- Litigation Analytics for judge, court, and attorney profiling
- KeyCite Overruling Risk warnings powered by machine learning
- WestSearch Plus with AI-ranked results

## Best For
- Litigation teams needing predictive analytics alongside research
- Firms requiring the most comprehensive legal database coverage

## Pricing
Enterprise pricing based on firm size. Individual plans start around $150/month. Full Westlaw Edge with AI features typically runs $300-$600/month per user. Volume discounts available.
