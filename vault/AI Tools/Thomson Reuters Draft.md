# Thomson Reuters Draft

Thomson Reuters Drafting Assistant (formerly Contract Express Drafting) brings AI-powered proofreading and validation to legal document creation. Integrated with Microsoft Word and connected to Westlaw's database, it checks citations for validity, identifies inconsistencies in defined terms and numbering, and flags potential drafting errors before documents are filed or executed.

## Features
- Westlaw-integrated citation checking with KeyCite signals
- Defined term and cross-reference validation
- Numbering and formatting error detection
- Court-specific filing format compliance
- Batch document checking capabilities

## Best For
- Firms in the Thomson Reuters ecosystem wanting seamless drafting support
- Litigation support teams responsible for document quality control

## Pricing
Included with Westlaw Edge subscriptions at certain tiers. Standalone at $85/month per user. Firm-wide licensing available.
