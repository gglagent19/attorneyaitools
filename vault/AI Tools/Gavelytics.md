# Gavelytics

Gavelytics focuses on an underserved area of legal analytics: state court data. While most analytics platforms concentrate on federal courts, Gavelytics provides detailed judge-level analytics for state courts, where the vast majority of litigation occurs. The platform tracks motion grant rates, case timelines, and judicial tendencies to help litigators make informed strategic decisions.

## Features
- State court judge analytics with motion grant rates
- Case timeline predictions by judge and case type
- Venue comparison tools for strategic filing decisions
- Motion-specific outcome analysis
- Judicial profile reports for case preparation

## Best For
- State court litigators developing motion strategy
- Attorneys making venue selection and forum shopping decisions

## Pricing
Individual plans from $125/month. Team plans from $100/month per user for 3+ users. Statewide access packages available at custom pricing.
