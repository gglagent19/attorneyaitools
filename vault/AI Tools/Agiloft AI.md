# Agiloft AI

Agiloft combines a highly configurable no-code platform with AI-powered contract analysis capabilities. The platform is known for its flexibility, allowing organizations to customize every aspect of their contract management workflows without coding. AI features include automatic clause extraction, risk scoring, and intelligent contract routing.

- No-code platform configuration for custom workflows
- AI-powered clause extraction and risk scoring
- Automated contract routing and approval chains
- Comprehensive audit trail and compliance tracking
- Integration with 100+ third-party applications

## Best For
