# Ravel Law

Ravel Law, now integrated into LexisNexis, provides visual legal analytics that help attorneys understand the relationships between cases, judges, and legal concepts. Its AI-powered visualization tools create interactive maps of case law, showing how authorities cite each other and revealing the most influential decisions in any area of law.

## Features
- Interactive case law visualization and citation mapping
- Judge analytics with ruling pattern analysis
- AI-powered identification of influential authorities
- Visual timeline of legal development in specific areas
- Integration with LexisNexis research platform

## Best For
- Litigation strategists analyzing judge behavior and preferences
- Legal researchers visualizing the development of legal doctrine

## Pricing
Available as part of LexisNexis subscriptions. Standalone access starting at $95/month. Enterprise pricing for firms requiring full analytics suite.
