# SpotDraft AI

SpotDraft is a modern CLM platform built for the speed of today's business environment. Its VerifAI feature uses AI to review contracts, flag risks, and suggest improvements based on the company's playbook. The platform is designed to reduce contract turnaround time while maintaining legal rigor, making it popular with technology companies and startups.

## Features
- VerifAI contract review engine with risk identification
- Dynamic template system for contract generation
- Editor with real-time redlining and collaboration
- Automated approval workflows with conditional routing
- Analytics dashboard for contract performance metrics

## Best For
- High-growth technology companies with increasing contract volumes
- Legal teams seeking to reduce contract cycle times

## Pricing
Starter plan for small teams from $1,500/month. Business plan from $3,500/month. Enterprise plan with custom integrations and dedicated support available.
