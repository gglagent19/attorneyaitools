# Solomonic

Solomonic specializes in legal analytics for UK commercial courts, filling a critical gap in the market. The platform uses AI to analyze decisions from courts in England and Wales, providing data on judicial behavior, case outcomes, and litigation trends. It is particularly valuable for commercial litigators handling disputes in London's courts, one of the world's most important dispute resolution centers.

## Features
- UK commercial court decision analytics
- Judicial behavior profiling for English judges
- Case outcome tracking by case type and value
- Costs analytics for litigation budgeting
- Law firm performance benchmarking in UK courts

## Best For
- Commercial litigators practicing in England and Wales
- International firms needing intelligence on UK court proceedings

## Pricing
Individual plans from GBP 150/month. Team plans from GBP 120/month per user. Firm-wide licensing available.
