# Judicata

Judicata takes a unique approach to legal research by mapping how courts actually apply legal standards. Rather than simply returning a list of cases, Judicata's AI analyzes judicial decisions to show how specific legal tests and standards have been interpreted and applied across different courts and fact patterns. This gives attorneys a deeper understanding of the legal landscape.

## Features
- Legal standard mapping across court decisions
- AI-powered brief scoring and gap analysis
- Judicial treatment analysis for specific legal issues
- Smart search with legal concept understanding
- Visual analytics of case law trends

## Best For
- Litigators preparing motions and needing to understand how courts apply standards
- Appellate attorneys analyzing trends in judicial reasoning
