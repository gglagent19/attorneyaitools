# Lawyaw

Lawyaw specializes in legal document automation, particularly court form filling and document assembly. The platform allows attorneys to create reusable templates that auto-populate with client data, dramatically reducing the time spent on repetitive paperwork. It supports state-specific court forms and integrates with practice management tools for seamless data flow.

## Features
- Automated court form filling for state-specific forms
- Custom template creation with merge fields
- Client intake form integration
- Batch document generation for multiple matters
- Integration with Clio, MyCase, and other PM tools

## Best For
- Family law, estate planning, and immigration attorneys with repetitive forms
- Solo and small firm practitioners seeking document automation

## Pricing
Free tier with limited templates. Professional plan at $49/month. Firm plan at $39/month per user for 3+ users. Annual billing saves 20%.
