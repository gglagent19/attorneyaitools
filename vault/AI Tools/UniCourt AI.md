# UniCourt AI

UniCourt provides a modern, AI-enhanced interface for accessing and analyzing court records across federal and state jurisdictions. The platform normalizes court data from disparate sources, making it searchable and analyzable. Its enterprise API is particularly popular with legal technology companies, insurance carriers, and corporate legal departments that need to integrate court data into their own systems.

## Features
- Normalized court data across 400+ state and federal courts
- AI-powered party and attorney matching across cases
- Real-time case tracking and alert notifications
- Enterprise API for legal data integration
- Analytics dashboards for litigation trends

## Best For
- Legal technology companies needing court data APIs
- Corporate legal departments tracking litigation exposure

## Pricing
Free basic access with limited searches. Professional plan at $99/month. Enterprise API pricing based on volume, starting at $500/month.
