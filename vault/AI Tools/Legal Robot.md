# Legal Robot

Legal Robot uses machine learning to analyze legal documents, providing readability scores, identifying unusual or potentially risky clauses, and translating legal language into plain English. The platform is designed to make legal documents more transparent and accessible, helping both legal professionals and consumers understand what they are agreeing to.

## Features
- AI-powered legal document readability scoring
- Unusual clause detection and risk flagging
- Plain language translation of legal text
- Comparison against standard clause libraries
- API for integrating analysis into other platforms

## Best For
- Legal teams reviewing high volumes of standard contracts
- Businesses wanting to ensure their terms and policies are readable

## Pricing
Free tier with basic analysis of up to 5 documents per month. Professional plan at $49/month with advanced analysis. Enterprise API access starting at $299/month.
