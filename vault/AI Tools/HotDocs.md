# HotDocs

HotDocs is one of the most established names in legal document automation, now enhanced with AI capabilities. The platform excels at generating complex legal documents using sophisticated conditional logic, calculations, and data integration. Used by major law firms, government agencies, and corporations, HotDocs handles document assembly at enterprise scale.

## Features
- Advanced conditional logic for complex document assembly
- AI-powered template creation from existing documents
- Multi-format output (Word, PDF, HTML)
- Enterprise-grade security and compliance
- Integration with document management and CRM systems

## Best For
- Large law firms with complex, high-volume document needs
- Government agencies automating public-facing legal forms
