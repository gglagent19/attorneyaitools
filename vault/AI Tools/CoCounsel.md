# CoCounsel

CoCounsel, developed by Casetext and now part of Thomson Reuters, is one of the most capable AI legal assistants available. Built on GPT-4 and fine-tuned for legal work, CoCounsel can draft legal documents, conduct research, review documents for key information, prepare deposition outlines, and create timelines from case files. It combines multiple legal AI capabilities in a single platform.

## Features
- Legal document drafting with citation support
- Document review for specific issues or provisions
- Deposition preparation with suggested questions
- Timeline generation from uploaded case documents
- Research memos with verified legal citations

## Best For
- Litigation teams needing a versatile AI assistant across tasks
- Attorneys wanting a single platform for research, drafting, and review

## Pricing
Available as part of Casetext subscriptions starting at $250/month per user. Enterprise pricing through Thomson Reuters for firm-wide deployment.
