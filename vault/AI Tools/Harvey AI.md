# Harvey AI

Harvey AI is a generative AI platform purpose-built for legal professionals. Developed in partnership with OpenAI and trained on a vast corpus of legal data, Harvey assists attorneys with research, drafting, and analysis tasks. The platform is trusted by leading Am Law 100 firms and delivers remarkably accurate, jurisdiction-aware legal insights.

## Features
- Natural language legal research with cited case law and statutes
- Legal memo and brief drafting with jurisdiction-specific formatting
- Contract analysis and clause extraction
- Multi-jurisdictional regulatory research
- Custom fine-tuning for firm-specific workflows

## Best For
- Large law firms needing AI-augmented legal research at scale
- In-house counsel performing cross-jurisdictional regulatory analysis
