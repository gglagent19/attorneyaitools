# Contract Express

Contract Express by Thomson Reuters is a document automation solution that transforms complex legal templates into easy-to-use questionnaires. Business users can generate accurate, compliant first drafts without legal training, while lawyers maintain control over the underlying templates and logic. The platform reduces drafting time by up to 80% for routine documents.

## Features
- Intelligent template builder with conditional logic
- Self-service questionnaires for business users
- Clause library management and version control
- Batch document generation capabilities
- Integration with iManage and other DMS platforms

## Best For
- Law firms standardizing document output across practice groups
- Corporate legal departments enabling business self-service

## Pricing
Starting at $60/user/month for cloud deployment. Enterprise licensing available. On-premise options for firms with specific security requirements.
