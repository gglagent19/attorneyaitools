# Claimant Keyword Map — Shielded SEO (Week 1, Day 1)

The directory's existing SEO targets **attorneys**. Shielded's buyer is a **policyholder with a denied or underpaid claim**. This map repoints the programmatic + content engine at *that* searcher.

> Volume figures are directional (use DataForSEO/GSC to confirm). What matters is **intent**: every term below is someone mid-problem, ready to act — exactly when the free 90-second analysis converts.

---

## Do / Don't

- **Do** lead with the searcher's pain in the first 60 words; put the free-analysis CTA above the fold.
- **Do** keep every page genuinely useful (real steps, real DOI link) — these are YMYL; thin pages get filtered.
- **Don't** state specific per-state statutory deadlines we haven't verified — point to the state Dept of Insurance instead.
- **Don't** imply legal representation or public adjusting. Position as a self-help analysis/drafting tool.
