## Posting cadence (suggested)

- **Mon** Reel 5 (emotional / wellness — strongest hook for shares)
- **Tue** Reel 3 (associate-targeted — peak LinkedIn engagement)
- **Wed** Reel 4 (procurement/ops — Wednesday is decision-maker day)
- **Thu** Reel 1 (BigLaw burnout — Thursday-night engagement)
- **Fri** Reel 2 (consumer-facing — Friday/weekend search intent)

Cross-post all 5 to: Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn (with branded text overlay), X (vertical video).

## Production notes

- Every image has emerald + navy accents to match the site's design system
- All hero scenes intentionally show outcomes (the smile, the relief, the family) not the product — brand-awareness, not feature ads
- 3:4 aspect ratio works on every short-video surface (IG Reels, TikTok, Shorts, LinkedIn)
- Add the logo bug bottom-right + URL bottom-center in CapCut/Premiere overlay
