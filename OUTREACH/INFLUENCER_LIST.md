## Engagement Cadence (90 Days)

- **Weeks 1–2:** Follow all 20. Turn on notifications. Read last 10 posts each to calibrate tone.
- **Weeks 3–6:** Leave 2–3 substantive comments per influencer per week. No self-promotion.
- **Weeks 7–9:** Share 1–2 of their posts weekly with added commentary and original insight.
- **Weeks 10–12:** Begin direct outreach — ask for quotes, pitch guest content, or propose collaborations based on the relationship built during weeks 1–9.

## General Rules

- Never pitch cold; always engage first.
- Credit and tag thoughtfully — not as bait.
- Lead with value (data, research, useful framing) before any ask.
- Disclose AttorneyAITools affiliation transparently in every interaction.
