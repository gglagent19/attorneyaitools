# Backlink Targets — 30 High-Value Opportunities

Thirty prioritized backlink targets across legal directories, AI tool directories, and niche/industry sources. DA estimates are approximate and should be re-verified before outreach.

---

## Section A — Legal Directories (10)

### 1. Avvo
- **URL:** https://www.avvo.com
- **Submission process:** Primarily an attorney profile directory, but business profiles for legal services can be created. Claim a listing as "AttorneyAITools" under legal services / legal technology and link from the profile.
- **Estimated DA:** 88
- **Estimated time:** 1–2 hours

### 2. Lawyers.com (Martindale-Nolo network)
- **URL:** https://www.lawyers.com
- **Submission process:** Submit via their Resource / Legal Services listings. For editorial links, pitch a guest article to Nolo's legal tech vertical; Nolo syndicates to Lawyers.com.
- **Estimated DA:** 86
- **Estimated time:** 3–4 hours

### 3. Justia
- **URL:** https://www.justia.com
- **Submission process:** Submit to Justia's Legal Web Directory (https://dir.justia.com) under "Legal Technology" or "Legal Resources." Free listing with backlink.
- **Estimated DA:** 89
- **Estimated time:** 30 minutes

### 4. FindLaw (Thomson Reuters)
- **URL:** https://www.findlaw.com
- **Submission process:** No public directory submission. Pursue editorial contribution via the FindLaw "Legal Technology" blog or Corporate Counsel section. Pitch a guest article.
- **Estimated DA:** 91
- **Estimated time:** 6–10 hours (pitching + writing)

### 5. HG.org Legal Resources
- **URL:** https://www.hg.org
- **Submission process:** Submit a legal resource listing at https://www.hg.org/suggest.html. Also offers paid and free article submissions.
- **Estimated DA:** 72
- **Estimated time:** 1 hour

### 6. Law.com
- **URL:** https://www.law.com
- **Submission process:** Editorial only. Pitch a contributed piece to LegalTech News (ALM) or the Law.com commentary section.
- **Estimated DA:** 88
- **Estimated time:** 8–12 hours

### 7. ABA Legal Technology Resource Center (ABA LTRC)
- **URL:** https://www.americanbar.org/groups/departments_offices/legal_technology_resources/
- **Submission process:** Offer to contribute a column or tool roundup to Law Technology Today (https://www.lawtechnologytoday.org). Pitch via the LTRC editor.
- **Estimated DA:** 92
- **Estimated time:** 10–15 hours

### 8. National Law Review
- **URL:** https://www.natlawreview.com
- **Submission process:** Become a contributing author; submit legal tech articles with author bio containing link. Free tier available.
- **Estimated DA:** 84
- **Estimated time:** 4–6 hours per article

### 9. Super Lawyers
- **URL:** https://www.superlawyers.com
- **Submission process:** Not a directory submission; pursue editorial mention via their "Legal Tips" or resource center. Alternatively, target their news feed with a press release.
- **Estimated DA:** 85
- **Estimated time:** 3–5 hours

### 10. Legal Reader
- **URL:** https://www.legalreader.com
- **Submission process:** Accepts contributed articles and press releases. Submit via their contact page with a legal tech pitch.
- **Estimated DA:** 62
- **Estimated time:** 2 hours

## Section C — Niche & Industry Sources (10)

### 21. r/Lawyertalk (Reddit)
- **URL:** https://www.reddit.com/r/Lawyertalk
- **Submission process:** Participate genuinely. Share AttorneyAITools resources only when directly answering questions; avoid self-promotion violations. Build karma first.
- **Estimated DA:** 91 (Reddit site-wide)
- **Estimated time:** Ongoing — 30 min/week

### 22. LinkedIn — Legal Innovators / Legal Ops groups
- **URL:** https://www.linkedin.com (search "Legal Innovators," "Corporate Legal Ops Consortium")
- **Submission process:** Join groups, comment thoughtfully, post category roundups linking to AttorneyAITools resources.
- **Estimated DA:** 98 (LinkedIn site-wide)
- **Estimated time:** Ongoing — 1 hour/week

### 23. Lawyerist Insider Community
